Shrimp Gazpacho

Healthy and refreshing cold shrimp soup ideal for summer days. This tasty soup can be served with nice salty crackers.

Serves 6

Cook time: 30min

Easy

Ingredients:

  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cup of tomato juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • Vegetable broth powder
  • 900gr of cooked shrimps
  • 1 tablespoon of crushed garlic
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped coriander
  • 1/4 cup of ketchup
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice
  • A few drops of maggy sauce
  • 1 chopped cucumber
  • 2 chopped avocados
  • Salty crackers (optional*)

Method:

01 Place the shrimp in a large bowl.

02 Stir garlic, onion, coriander and add vegetable broth powder to taste.

03 Mix in tomato juice, ketchup, lemon juice, maggy sauce and sugar. Add salt and pepper to taste.

04 Mix in the cooked shrimps, chopped tomatoes, chopped cucumber and chopped avocados.

05 Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours

06 Serve in a large bowl or ladle into individual bowls.
