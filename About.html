Food waste in the UK


Hundreds of thousands of perfectly good food is wasted by the food industry in the UK each year (1.9 million tonnes by the food industry and 600,000 tonnes from restaurants alone), costing billions of pounds and leaving more people hungry than ever. Whilst there are organisations in the UK directly working towards limiting food industry waste, by redistributing food industry surplus, to providing food takeaway boxes, here at The Recipe Lab we aim to provide solutions to limit food waste right here, in our own kitchens. We believe that the tiny changes that we make in our shopping baskets, will benefit us is more ways than we know, by:


  • Limiting damage to our environment
  • Saving money by cutting unnecessary costs
  • Being conscious of those who are less fortunate than us

By providing healthy, delicious recipes based on what is in your fridge, rather than ingredients that are yet to be bought, we believe that we can collectively work towards a better world where we are able to enjoy food in a healthy, conscious manner. Join us in our cause and let's save food!
